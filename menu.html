About Famoso Neapolitan Pizzeria


Tradition since 1995

We love to be the neighbourhood pizzeria that everyone comes to. At Famoso Guildford, you’ll find a fun and lively atmosphere for you to relax with your friends and family any day of the week. There are plenty of choices in our menu – from Neapolitan pizzas to new world pizzas, artisan pastas, craft beers, craft cocktails and friendly, energetic staff to make your visit memorable.
